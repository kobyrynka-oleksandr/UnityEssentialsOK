About audio at 3D Essentials scene:

Added: background music, ball bounce and wooden bricks colliding sound, clock chime sound(activation with the left mouse button in the trigger zone near the clock).

Added PlaySoundOnCollision, Clock and ClockPlaySoundTrigger scripts.
PlaySoundOnCollision: Used to play sound when objects collide (Ball and Wooden bricks).
Clock: Determines the clock with the audio source attached and method for playing sound.
ClockPlaySoundTrigger: Defines a trigger zone within which, if the player is inside it and the left mouse button is pressed, a method for playing the clock sound is called.
